MONSTER DEADEYE: Shooting Game for PC 💥

Monster Deadeye is a 3d shooting game for PC where you have to shoot monsters coming your way.

Important Note:

A beep sound will come whenever a monster is near you.
You have an advanced Sniper gun (press Q for Sniper game)
There are only 4 sniper bullets for you, through which you can shoot the enemies in 1 shot.
Your objective is to kill all the monsters and save your health.
You require 2 shots to kill Tomato Monster and Gun Man, and 5-6 shots to kill Mega Monster.
You can see the positions of monsters through the 2D mini-map
